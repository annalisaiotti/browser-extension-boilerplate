Nomoremoji is a web extension designed to counteract the impoverishment of language caused by the disproportionate use of emojis. Once installed, this extension will allow you to translate the emojis on your screen with some linguistic expressions useful for interpreting the true meaning of the quote.

TRY IT NOW!

  1. Download
  2. Select your language to translate the emojis (now only in italian)
  3. You will immediatly see how these emoji will turn into text:
